About
Add headshot

Two decades at the bench
and the board table.

I began my career in medicinal chemistry — synthesising lead compounds, running ADMET screens, watching promising molecules fail in Phase II for reasons that had nothing to do with the science. That gap between discovery and decision-making is what I've spent my career closing.

Over twenty years I've worked across pharma, early-stage biotech, and life sciences investment — as an operator, a scientific advisor, and an investor. Today I work directly with founders, clinical leaders, and institutional investors who need someone who can read a crystallography report and a 10-K in the same afternoon.

My clients include Series A biotech founders who need a seasoned co-thinker on development strategy, and European family offices and VC funds who require a credible scientific lens before committing capital to complex biology.

One firm. No junior staff. Every engagement receives my full, undivided attention. I take a selective number of mandates each year precisely because depth of engagement is the only thing I sell.
